1. Change project name; this is your artifactID
2. Go to pom.xml and change artifactID (project name) and groupID (com.bibhash.artifactID)
3. Change your app's name, description, version
4. Near end of pom.xml Change <mainClass>yourGroupIDTheOneWithCom.Main</mainClass> (replace with your groupId.Main)
5. Refactor and rename package name to groupID
6. In Main.fxml change fx:controller="yourGroupIDTheOneWithCom.Controller"> (replace with your groupId.Main)
7. Clean, install, run with Goal: compile javafx:run
